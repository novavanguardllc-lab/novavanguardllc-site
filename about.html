Our story

One person, two crafts,
one standard.

Nova Vanguard LLC started as a way to build the tools its founder wished existed — for creators, and for people tired of news that picks a side before it picks its facts.

Why we exist

Nova Vanguard LLC is founded and run by Tremaine McCullough, built on nights and weekends around a full-time job. It spans two things that turned out to share the same DNA: software that helps creators do better work in their own voice, and independent political commentary that treats the audience as capable of drawing their own conclusions.

How we work

Receipts first

Every claim gets sourced. Every source gets checked. No outlet gets a pass just because it's popular.

Independent, genuinely

No straight-ticket voting, no default allegiance. Every story is judged on its own evidence.

Creator-owned voice

Our software drafts, checks, and researches — but the voice on screen is always the creator's own.

Where to find us

New coverage runs daily as Shorts, with nightly recaps and a weekly wrap-up every Friday, primarily on Newsbreak and YouTube under the Tremaine McCullough News Network.
