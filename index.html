Nova Vantage

Nova Vantage is a desktop and mobile application for YouTube content creators. It connects to a creator's own YouTube account to help them research verified news sources, draft video scripts in their own voice, generate SEO-optimized titles and descriptions, fact-check claims before publishing, and track their channel's performance over time.

What Nova Vantage Does

  • Reads a signed-in creator's own YouTube channel and video data to display performance analytics
  • Generates video scripts and titles based on the creator's input and past content style
  • Fact-checks claims a creator plans to include in a video
  • Does not post, upload, or publish anything on the creator's behalf without their direct action

What Nova Vantage Does Not Do

  • No AI-generated voiceovers
  • No AI-generated video or avatars
  • No selling or sharing of a creator's YouTube data with third parties

Your Data

Nova Vantage requests access to YouTube Data API v3 information only to power the features listed above. Access can be revoked at any time from the app's Settings screen or from Google Account Permissions.
